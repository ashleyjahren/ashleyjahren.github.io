VERA

a project to help liver transplant patients with type II diabetes.

VERA is a Design for America project started in July 2015. We are working with a research team at Northwestern Medicine to improve the transition of care for liver transplant patients who get type 2 diabetes post-operation.

How Can We...

help liver transplant patients with Type II Diabetes manage their condition and transition to independence?

An Ecosystem of Design Solutions

Penfo is an insulin pen that holds dosage and type of medication information on a pull out pen sleeve, and

The Sidekit is a hospital to home transitional kit that helps users learn how and when to take their new medications and better utilize their equipment.

Try out our App

An interactive prototype

VERA is an interactive application that motivates users to continue living a healthy lifestyle and manage their condition by competing anonymously against other players in their community.
