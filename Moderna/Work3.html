Despicable Me in Space

This game was built entirely in IA-32 Assembly language over the course of 10 weeks for EECS 205.

Modes of Playing

You are Kevin, one of Gru's minions. El Macho has turned all of your friends into wild purple minions that eat everything in their path (including you if they get the chance)! Gru has tasked you with controlling the stampede and turning as many of the purple minions back to yellow. Your ultimate goal is to shoot at the purple minions with Doctor Nefario's Jelly Antidote by pressing the spacebar. But, be careful don't let the purple minions hit you first...

Key Challenges

  • Debugging

    The only way to debug the program was to run the game. When I broke the code, it would display a black screen instead of the actual video game. This made the process much more time consuming and frustrating when I couldn't find an error that I was looking for.

  • Time Consuming

    I built this project from the ground up using no libraries. I created and loaded images myself, drew the background with pixels, and created object rotation functionality with a separate file.
